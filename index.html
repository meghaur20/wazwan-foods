About us

Wazwan foods was started by professional chefs and IIT alumni in 2012 and since the inception the focus was to find the best dishes from several cusine under one roof. We have developed several spice mixture and compound combinations for several dishes and made the cooking process easy.

The biggest problem in restaurant industry is the chef and cooks. Hiring and retention is a real challenge and to overcome all the menace we have developed compound based fixed proportions cooking methods. Freshers can be trained and hired to cook intricate dishes thereby reducing the cost of staff and wastage. Indian curry, Tandoor, indi chinese, Mughlai, Kashmiri, Continental, Fast food, Cocktail and mocktail, Desserts, Coffee bar etc
